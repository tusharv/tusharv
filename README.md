# Hi, I'm Tushar Vaghela

### Building products that matter. Leading teams that deliver.

I'm a **Director of Engineering at Proximity Works**, based in Mumbai, India. I started my journey as a hands-on engineer and have grown into a technology leader who enjoys taking ideas from whiteboard discussions to production systems that scale, perform, and create real user impact.

I work at the intersection of **engineering leadership, AI-driven platforms, distributed systems, product development, and developer productivity**. Over the years, I have built and led products across **media, gaming, e-learning, advertising technology, SaaS, and online services**.

My current focus is on building high-performing engineering teams, architecting scalable systems, mentoring developers, and using AI thoughtfully to improve how products are imagined, built, and delivered.
